SKYOM City.

A 50 acres integrated township in Mohanlalganj, Lucknow which combines world-class amenities, green spaces, and architectural brilliance.

Scroll

WHERE ELEGANCE MEETS UNRIVALED TOWNSHIP LUXURY

SKYOM City is a masterfully planned 50-acres residential township located in Lucknow's fast-developing corridor — Mohanlalganj. Designed to offer a harmonious blend of nature, modern infrastructure, and vibrant community living, the township features segmented luxury villa plots, 24+ themed parks, and dedicated zones for education and wellness.

With extra-wide boulevards, zero-congestion internal layouts, and lush linear parks, every day begins immersed in green tranquillity while keeping urban conveniences within arm's reach.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SKYOM City 50-acres township, RERA approval, and plot availability.

Is SKYOM City a RERA registered township? +
Yes, SKYOM City is a fully RERA-approved integrated township under registration number UPRERAPRJ600292/06/2025. All development adheres to regulatory statutory standards.
What plot sizes are available in SKYOM City? +
SKYOM City offers premium villa residential plots ranging from 1,500 Sq. Ft., 1,800 Sq. Ft., 2,100 Sq. Ft. up to 3,200 Sq. Ft., featuring wide 40-ft and 60-ft internal avenue access.
Where is SKYOM City located in Lucknow? +
SKYOM City is located in Mohanlalganj, Lucknow — one of the fastest growing urban corridors with seamless connection to Shaheed Path Expressway, Ekana Stadium, and Lucknow Airport.
