Where Nature Meets Luxury

Skyom City is a thoughtfully planned residential township located in one of Lucknow's most promising and fast-developing corridors — Mohanlalganj. Designed to offer a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and community living, the project features segmented residential plots, a luxury clubhouse, themed parks, and dedicated space for educational and healthcare centers.

It is not just a place to live but a lifestyle revolution. The numerous luscious green parks, wide traffic-calmed roads, and modern lifestyle amenities will elevate your living standard, offering resort-style features right at home. With an unwavering commitment to quality and execution, Skyom City is where the future begins.
