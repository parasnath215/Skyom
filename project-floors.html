THE GROVE AT SKYOM FLOORS

SKYOM Floors.

Premium independent floors designed with meticulous attention to detail, offering spacious estate living in an exclusive private enclave.

Scroll

WHERE LUXURY FLOWS IN ITS PUREST FORM

The Grove is an exclusive enclave of luxury independent villas and floors, offering expansive estate homes shaped by landscape, light, and privacy. Embedded within SKYOM Sanctuary's blue-green network, the neighbourhood is defined by flowing waterways, linear parks, and shaded pedestrian routes that guide daily movement.

Homes are thoughtfully positioned away from high-activity zones, with layered landscaping preserving a sense of quiet while remaining seamlessly connected to cycling paths, wellness loops, and neighbourhood amenities. Here, living feels grounded, generous, and naturally private.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SKYOM Floors, layout specifications, and site visits.

What configurations are available at SKYOM Floors? +
SKYOM Floors features spacious 4 BR, 5 BR, and 6 BR independent estate floors with saleable areas ranging from 4,905 Sq. Ft. up to 6,950 Sq. Ft. All units include private elevators, guest suites, and servant quarters.
Is SKYOM Floors a low-density private enclave? +
Yes, SKYOM Floors is designed as an exclusive low-density enclave surrounded by linear parks and water features to maximize privacy and quietness for every homeowner.
What amenities are included in the community? +
Residents enjoy access to 12+ world-class amenities including a massive clubhouse, yoga and meditation decks, cycling networks, sports courts, beach lagoon, and forest play parks.
